Universal Satellite Network Infrastructure Project Opportunity
I am Felipe Bascou, writing to you on behalf of Born2Global, the Korean government's global deep tech startup incubation and acceleration agency under the Korean Ministry of Science and ICT. <br><br>
Born2Global aims to help Korean deep tech companies expand to the global market with projects and partnerships around the world. We are always looking for potential matching partnerships between Korean deep tech startups and foreign companies. <br><br>
We have found a matching opportunity that might interest your company, and the foreign company profile is as follows: <br><br>
A company from Argentina is looking for a Korean partnership in the aerospace sector to help develop a Universal Satellite Network Infrastructure for data acquisition and communications. The aforementioned Argentinian company is searching for a Korean counterpart working in the fields of: <br>
<ul>
    <li>Space-qualified parts for CubeSats, PocketQube and microSats, like structural frames, joints, bolts, hinges, deploying mechanisms, etc.</li>
    <li>Electronic boards and flexible substrate's electronics and conductors</li>
    <li>Actuators, magnetorquers, coils, batteries, nozzles, little tanks and propulsion systems, solar panels, antennas, RF components, and many more, having in mind that satellites will be different from each other, or there will be groups with similarities.</li>
    <li>Antennas and Ground Stations' equipment</li>
    <li>On board computers</li>
    <li>Simulation hardware and software, to build HIL (Hardware In The Loop) simulators with whole system orbit propagators, node intelligence simulators, DTN simulators including ISL comm windows and path time optimization, and subsystem functions simulations.</li>
    <li>Cleanroom good suppliers</li>
    <li>Launch systems providers or brokers</li>
</ul>
If you are interested in this project opportunity, we would be happy to send you the company and project information/details. Furthermore, we could also help you organize a meeting with the company. If you have any questions or concerns, please don't hesitate to ask as I will gladly assist.<br><br>
Thank you for your time!<br><br>
